Blackjack


The objective is to have a hand value of 21 or as close to it as possible without exceeding it. The player can choose to hit (receive another card) or stand (keep their current hand) until they reach a hand value they are comfortable with. The dealer will hit until their hand value is 17 or higher.


Scoring:

  • Cards 2 through 10 are worth their face value.
  • Jacks, Queens, and Kings are worth 10.
  • Aces are worth 1 or 11, whichever is more advantageous to the player.
  • A Blackjack is a hand that consists of an Ace and a ten-point card (10, Jack, Queen, or King) and is worth 21 points.
  • A Bust is a hand that exceeds 21 points.
  • A Blackjack is an automatic win, unless the dealer also has a Blackjack, in which case it is a tie.
